## 🏗️ Building a Pro AI Research Dashboard with Gemini & Chrome Side Panel

I just finished building a dedicated **AI Research Repository** Chrome Extension! 🚀

The goal was simple: Create a high-fidelity, persistent dashboard that curates the latest and most influential AI technical papers directly in my browser side panel.

### 🧠 Powered by Gemini
The engine uses a fallback chain of Google's latest models to ensure I always get the most accurate research abstracts:
✅ **Gemini 2.5 Flash** (Primary)
✅ **Gemini 2.0 Flash**
✅ **Gemini 1.5 Flash** (Stable fallback)

### ✨ Key Features:
- **3-Tier Learning**: Papers categorized into Novice, Builder, and Expert levels.
- **Top 10 Curation**: Automatically fetches the 10 most recent papers for each tier.
- **Pure Research**: Direct abstracts with full metadata (Authors + Publication Dates)—no conversational synthesis, just the technical core.
- **Glassmorphism UI**: A sleek, dark-themed dashboard that stays open on the side while I browse.

### 🛠️ The Tech Journey:
This started as a simple experiment and evolved through several iterations:
1. **Side Panel API**: Integrated for a persistent, non-intrusive UI.
2. **Background Workers**: Migrated all API logic to service workers for maximum reliability.
3. **Model Orchestration**: Built a custom retry system to handle API availability seamlessly.
